The Recipient shall not, directly or indirectly, reverse-engineer, decompile,

the Recipient creates relating to Imara, or that incorporates the Confidential
Information, **vests in and is hereby irrevocably assigned to the Discloser** upon
creation. The Recipient waives any moral rights to the extent permitted by law and will,
on request, sign any document needed to perfect the Discloser's ownership.

Nothing in this agreement grants the Recipient any licence or right in the Confidential
Information or in Imara, except the limited right to use it for the stated purpose. All
rights are reserved by the Discloser.

## 6. Return / Destruction
On the Discloser's request or on termination, the Recipient will return or permanently
destroy all Confidential Information (and copies) and certify destruction in writing.

## 7. Term & Survival
Confidentiality obligations survive termination and continue for as long as the
information remains a trade secret / not publicly known, without time limit.

## 8. Remedies
The Recipient agrees that breach would cause irreparable harm for which damages are
inadequate, and that the Discloser is entitled to **injunctive relief** in addition to
any other remedy, plus reasonable legal costs.
